🌈 A Multimedia Time Capsule

In the early days of Windows 95, tucked into the CD was a charming little video called goodtimes.avi, featuring Edie Brickell. Long before streaming was a thing, this video showed off the magic of multimedia—right on your desktop.

💾 Reflections from a Windows Insider

“I joined Microsoft in July 1998—fresh off my MCSE—with the privilege of supporting Windows 98’s release. But something about that hidden little video from Win95 always stuck with me… Edie Brickell singing in a soft red blouse, that sense of new horizons just beginning.”
